The Mechanic

The festival
is the game board.

Each stage has a unique cube. Visit a stage, get that cube. Trade with strangers to collect all six. Complete the set, bring it to the Cube booth. The cube gives people a reason to explore, approach, and connect.

Six stages. Six cubes.

Hero Products

Four cubes that
tell the story.

Beyond the stage cubes, these are the objects that define the Coachella × Cube experience.

The Volume

The Desert Cube

Clear resin with actual Coachella desert sand sealed inside. Literal "I was there" encased at palm scale. Different years use sand from different areas of the grounds. A time capsule of the festival, permanently preserved.

After Dark

The Sunset Cube

Glow-in-the-dark resin. Only available after sundown. Charges in daylight, glows through the night. Temporal scarcity — you can only get this cube when the sun is gone.

Golden Hour

The Mirror Cube

Polished stainless steel. Only available during golden hour, 5–7pm. Catches the desert light at the exact moment the festival transforms. The most photographed cube.

Installation

The Artist Cube

Each major art installation artist designs a cube in their installation's material. A pocket-scale version of a large-scale work. The art you can take home.

The Two-Weekend Advantage

The game doesn't end
when the festival does.

Two weekends create a mechanic Art Basel didn't have. Some cubes only exist on one weekend. Completing a full set means crossing the weekend divide.

W1

Weekend One

Participants collect cubes, post on social media, start trading. Exclusive W1 editions create scarcity. The mechanic is new. The discovery is raw.

W2

Weekend Two

Attendees arrive already knowing the game. Participation rate 3–5x higher. Exclusive W2 editions complete the cross-weekend set. The game is pre-sold.

W1 posts cubes → W2 arrives informed → Higher participation → Cross-weekend trading → The game outlives the event
Brand Integration

Brands don't sponsor
the game. They enter it.

Every brand that activates at Coachella gets a cube in their signature material. Their activation becomes a pickup point. The cube IS the brand experience.

Collect brand cubes alongside stage cubes for the mega set. This isn't a logo placement. It's a participation mechanic that makes the brand part of the game.
